Our Roots

Happi's Nutri Foods was born from a deep passion for authentic African nutrition. Growing up in Port Harcourt, Ituma Miracle Happiness witnessed how modern processed foods were replacing the wholesome, nutrient dense meals of our ancestors. She knew something had to change.

The Vision

Miracle envisioned a brand that would reconnect Nigerians with the power of organic, locally sourced superfoods. Starting with Tom Brown the legendary energy porridge and premium non-GMO soybeans, Happi's Nutri Food quickly became synonymous with quality, purity, and trust.

Our Promise

Every product we offer is carefully sourced from trusted local farmers, processed without chemicals or additives, and delivered fresh to your doorstep. We believe that good nutrition is the foundation of a vibrant life, and we're committed to making it accessible to everyone.

When you choose Happi's Nutri Foods, you're not just buying a product you're joining a movement towards healthier, stronger Nigerian families.

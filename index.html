Who is Happi?

Ituma Miracle Happiness — fondly called "Happi" — grew up watching her grandmother blend grains by hand in their village kitchen. She saw how that simple porridge gave her family strength and vitality. When processed foods began dominating shelves, Miracle knew she had to preserve this legacy. Happi's Nutri Food was born to bring back the real, unadulterated African superfoods that built generations.

What Makes Us Different

✓ Farm-to-Table Transparency: We source directly from women-led cooperatives in Benue, Taraba, and Rivers State. Every grain is traceable.
✓ Zero Additives: No preservatives, no artificial flavors — just sun-dried, stone-ground goodness.
✓ Women-Powered: 80% of our production team are local women, empowering families.
✓ Proven Results: Trusted by over 500 families across Nigeria with consistent 5-star feedback.

When you buy from us, you're not just feeding your family — you're supporting a movement for authentic African nutrition.
